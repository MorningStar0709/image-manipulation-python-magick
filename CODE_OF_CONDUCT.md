# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Please keep this project welcoming, respectful, and focused on constructive technical collaboration.

Examples of positive behavior:

- asking questions in good faith
- giving actionable and specific feedback
- respecting different experience levels
- keeping discussions relevant to the project

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment or personal attacks
- hateful or discriminatory language
- repeated off-topic disruption
- sharing private information without permission

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove comments, issues, pull requests, or other contributions that violate these expectations.

Repeated or severe violations may lead to temporary or permanent blocking from project interactions.

## Reporting

If you encounter unacceptable behavior, contact the repository owner through GitHub with relevant context.
